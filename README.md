## 📌 Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) milestone project performed on a dataset of Bumble dating profiles. The objective of this analysis is to clean, process, and explore demographic trends, relationship orientations, and profile characteristics to extract meaningful behavioral insights about app users.

By leveraging Python's powerful data science stack, this project transforms raw profile records into visual summaries that highlight key user patterns.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings
* **Zodiac Sign Representation:** **Leo** and **Gemini** are consistently among the most represented astrological signs across almost all relationship status categories (*Single, Seeing Someone, Available*). These signs are traditionally associated with being social and outgoing, which likely explains their higher presence on a dating platform.
* **Underrepresented Demographics:** **Capricorn** is consistently the least represented zodiac sign across all major relationship statuses. This could be attributed to broader birth rate trends or specific demographic preferences regarding who chooses to download and engage with dating apps.
* **Proportional Trends:** Cross-tabulating relationship statuses and gender against zodiac signs reveals structured behaviors in how users present their profiles and personal details.
